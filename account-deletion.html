Account & Data Deletion

About Claude Learn accounts

Claude Learn does not have user accounts. The App is anonymous-by-design — we never ask for your name, email, phone number, or password, and you don't log in. There is nothing to "delete an account" because no account exists.

We do, however, retain a small amount of anonymous data tied to a randomly-generated device identifier, so that:

  • If you reinstall the App, your one-time in-app purchases (e.g. Course, Pro, Founder) can be restored.
  • We can improve the App using aggregated, anonymous usage analytics.

This page explains how to request deletion of that data.

What can be deleted

You may request deletion of any of the following at any time:

Data typeWhat it is
Purchase records Anonymous device ID + tier purchased + timestamp + payment session ID, used to restore your unlocks if you reinstall.
Product analytics events Anonymous interaction events (tab opens, lesson completions, paywall views) tied to the device UUID only, stored by our analytics provider PostHog (US).
Display name If you typed a name on the Welcome screen, it's stored on your device only — uninstalling the App removes it automatically. We never receive it.
Practice-chat messages Already not retained on our servers — your typed messages are transmitted to Anthropic, the reply is delivered, and the conversation is dropped. There is nothing for us to delete.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

How to request deletion

1
Find your anonymous device ID

Open the App and go to Settings → About → Debug info. Copy the UUID shown there (it looks something like 4f9a2c1b-8d3e-4f7c-b2a9-1e6f8c3d5a7b).

If you've already uninstalled the App, skip to step 2 — we can still locate your purchase records via the Google Play / Apple receipt you'd attach to your email.

2
Email us your deletion request

Send a short email from any inbox of your choice to latitude44@protonmail.com with the subject line Data deletion request and include:

  • Your anonymous device ID (from step 1), or
  • The Google Play / App Store / Stripe receipt for any in-app purchase you've made (we'll find your records from the order ID).
3
We confirm within 14 days

We'll reply confirming that the matching records have been permanently deleted from our backend database and from our analytics provider (PostHog). Where deletion through a third party (e.g. Google Play purchase history) is outside our control, we'll point you to the right contact.

Frequently asked

Can I just uninstall the App?

Uninstalling removes the App from your device, including any name you typed, settings, and progress saved locally. It does not automatically delete the small server-side purchase record or the analytics events tied to your anonymous device ID — use the email flow above to delete those too.

Will I lose my purchases?

Yes — once your purchase record is deleted, the restore-purchase mechanism won't be able to find it. If you reinstall the App afterwards, you'd need to make any purchases again. We recommend exporting your progress screenshot from the App first if you want to keep a record.

What's the difference between deletion and opt-out?

Deletion removes existing records. Opt-out stops future data collection. To opt out of analytics without deleting existing data, simply uninstall the App (which stops the App from firing further events). A future release may add an in-app opt-out toggle.

Note for Google Play reviewers and regulators: This page is the deletion contact channel referenced in our Privacy Policy (latitude44.app/privacy) and in our Play Store "Account deletion" declaration. Claude Learn does not use user accounts (anonymous-by-design), so there is no login portal — all deletion requests are handled by email per the process above. Response time is committed at 14 days and typically much faster.
